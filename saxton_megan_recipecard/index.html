Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2-¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup butter, at room temperature
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  3. Combine flour, baking soda and salt in medium bowl.
  4. Beat butter and granulated and brown sugars with electric mixer in large bowl until smooth and fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla; beat until well blended.
  5. Add flour mixture; beat until combined.
  6. Drop a spoonful of dough about 2 inches (5 cm) apart onto prepared baking sheets.
  7. Bake just until cookies lose their shiny appearance, about 9 minutes. Let cookies cool on baking sheet for 1 minute before removing to wire rack to cool completely.

Why This Dish Is My Favourite

These cookies are my favourite dish because they are so soft and gooey in the middle and crunchy on the outside. I love sweets and love the simple deliciousness of these cookies. They are also really simple to bake. They don’t take a long time to make which is great with my busy schedule. Also, pretty much everyone loves chocolate chip cookies so this is a great recipe to bake for others.
